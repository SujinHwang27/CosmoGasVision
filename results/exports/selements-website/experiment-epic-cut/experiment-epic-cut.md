### ep05 — per-pixel FGPA-tail regularizer ([D-41]; loss-per-pixel axis; degeneracy D2)

- **Identity**: per-voxel physics prior — penalize deviation of the network's (ρ, T) → n_HI relation from the FGPA scaling τ ∝ Δ^1.6 T^−0.7 (Hui-Gnedin 1997) on diffuse bins.
- **Problem fixed**: ep04's lesson — integrated statistics are gameable; a per-pixel constraint was believed immune.
- **The run**: 50-step host smoke only (P1, MLflow `21fb45cb…`); Tier-1 skipped per pre-committed stop.
- **Result of record**: **FAIL at smoke** — `loss_fgpa` descended 20× (6.37→0.31) but **mean_F_pred → 1.0000 exactly**: the network drove τ_pred → 0 everywhere via n_HI = ρ·X_HI → 0, evading the τ_amp anchor. ([D-42-meta] later corrected the mechanism reading to constant-prediction collapse: density flat ≈71.5, X_HI flat ≈3.3e-5 — D2.) Load-bearing architectural finding: **any regularizer evaluated only at the network's own predicted state is gameable when the data loss is weakly informative on the diffuse-bin majority** — this falsified ep04's own "per-pixel constraints are not gameable" redirect.
- **Problem raised**: regularize against ground-truth-anchored quantities, not self-consistent ones (→ ep06).
- **D-constituting**: [D-41]. **D-internal**: [D-42-meta] (retrospective that corrected the D2 narrative and inscribed the design-spec discipline rules).

## §2. Rulings on the six structural forks

**Fork 1 — P_F interventions: distinct epics (ep04–ep07), one each.** Each is a separately designed, dispatched, and pre-committedly retired experiment with its own D-XX and — the scientifically load-bearing point — its own *distinct degeneracy signature* (D1 amplitude-shrink / D2 constant-prediction / D3 asymmetric-head / D4 trivial-collapse-with-active-embedding). Binding caption for the group: four specific interventions on a falsification queue produced four distinct degeneracy signatures — never "the cascade proves nothing can work."

**Fork 2 — trainability chain: ONE grouped epic (ep08), not omitted, not split.** "Neither has a clean result" is wrong as a scientific read: the L1 arc's honest status is "instrument falsified, hypothesis narrowed" per run, but the *aggregate* — the 7-lever collapsed-basin cluster with its 0.89-decade variance band and the (b) datum (per-task balance fixed 4.4 decades, collapse unmoved) — IS a clean result of record ([D-63]/[D-65]), and the close-out spine counts these as interventions 5–12 of the paper's own table.
